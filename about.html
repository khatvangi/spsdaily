About SPS Daily

SPS Daily is a curated digest of the best writing on science, philosophy, and society. In an age of infinite content and algorithmic feeds, we believe in human curation — the careful selection of ideas that matter.

"Ekam Sat, viprāḥ bahudhā vadanti"
Truth is one; the wise speak of it in many ways.
— Rigveda 1.164.46

What We Curate

  • Science — Not just discoveries, but what they mean. Physics, biology, neuroscience, and the methods of knowing.
  • Philosophy — Ancient and modern. Ethics, metaphysics, philosophy of mind, and the examined life.
  • Society — How we live together. Technology's impact, institutions, culture, and the future we're building.

What We Avoid

We have no patience for pseudoscience, conspiracy thinking, or the confident certainty of ideologues. We seek writers who are rigorous yet humble, who follow evidence where it leads, who can hold complexity without collapsing into simplicity.

Inspiration

SPS Daily is inspired by Arts & Letters Daily, which has curated the humanities with distinction since 1998. We aim to do the same for the sciences and the questions that bridge disciplines.

No Algorithms

Every link is chosen by a human reader. We use AI as a tool to discover and summarize, but the final selection reflects human judgment about what's genuinely worth your time.
